# Save the Green 🌍🌱

**Save the Green** is a mobile application concept and startup project aimed at promoting environmental sustainability by incentivizing eco-friendly daily habits. This repository contains both the strategic business documentation and a functional Android prototype (MVP) to demonstrate the core features of the app.

## 🚀 The Vision
The core objective of *Save the Green* is to bridge the gap between environmental awareness and actionable, everyday behaviors. Through gamification, community engagement, and strategic partnerships, the app transforms sustainability into an accessible and rewarding lifestyle.

## 📁 Repository Structure & Deliverables

### 1. Pitch & Vision (`/01-Pitch`)
* **Project Vision & Goal:** A concise executive summary of the mission, the problem we are solving, and our proposed solution.
* **App Pitch Deck:** A visual presentation explaining the app's functionality, user journey, and core features.

### 2. Business Strategy (`/02-Business-Strategy`)
* **Business Model & Value Proposition Canvas:** Visual frameworks detailing our customer segments, key activities, cost structure, and the unique value we deliver to our users and eco-partners.
* **Comprehensive Business Plan:** A detailed document covering market analysis, go-to-market strategy, marketing initiatives, and long-term financial viability.

### 3. Android Prototype (`/03-Android-Prototype`)
* **`SaveTheGreen-Prototype.apk`:** A functional Minimum Viable Product (MVP) of the application built for Android devices.

## 📱 How to Test the Prototype
To install and test the app on your Android device:
1. Download the `SaveTheGreen-Prototype.apk` file from the `/03-Android-Prototype` folder.
2. Transfer the file to your Android smartphone.
3. Locate the file using your phone's File Manager and tap it to install.
   * *Note: You may need to enable "Install unknown apps" from your device's security settings to sideload the application.*
4. Open the app and explore the eco-friendly features!
